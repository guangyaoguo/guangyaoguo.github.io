It's been a wonderful experience delving into theoretical computer science during my honours year, fourturnately guided by dedicated mentor [Dr. André van Renssen](https://andrevanrenssen.droppages.com/) at Sydney Algorithms and Computing Theory Group ([SACT](https://sydneyalgorithms.wordpress.com/)). With a strong passion for bridging theory and application, I am committed to contributing to computer science research and pursuing a Ph.D. degree in the years to come.
